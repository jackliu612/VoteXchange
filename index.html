About VoteXchange

Ever been disappointed with the outcome of an election?
Think the country is headed in the wrong direction?

You're not alone! VoteXchange is a free online service that seeks to connect citizens to opportunities in their communities.VoteXchange rewards you for political activism using a blockchain-based point system. Many of these activities you may already be doing!
  • VOTE!
  • Donating to community or political organizations
  • Volunteering in the community
  • Attending town halls or other community meetings
  • Writing to or calling elcted officials
  • Attending political rallies
Points can be verified and added to your account through events with registered sponsors. VoteXchange then allows you to redeem points for prizes of your choice.

Your participation in the community is valuable, We're here to recognize your deeds. Sign up today!

Become a VoteXchange Member Organization!

VoteXchange wouldn't be possible without the support of every organization that registers with us. Whether you're organizing a rally or representing city hall, let us know! Just submit a form with the details of your event and we'll take care of the rest: giving points to attendees and advertising on our homepage Let's work together to create real change.
